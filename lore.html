Venrora Yesdi is a dryad, who was born in Africa, and sent away by her parents for her protection. She was found off the coast of the Awakened Outback in Australia by a man she lovingly dubbed 'Redback'- because he was always very sunburned.

Growing up in the Awakened Outback was nerve-wracking, but she learned quickly how to protect herself from the wildlife- the nation's biggest threat at the time.

It was one day, though, that she found herself standing on a ship as it started sailing. Leaving Australia was an accident, but either way, she found herself standing at the port of a shipping company in Tenochtitlan, Aztl√°n.

It took a while, but eventually, she found a way to get comfortable in the new country. She was surprised though, to find a stranded child in the midst of the road. It was an easy decision, and Alcaudon joined her little journey to live a nice life.

At some point, Venrora realized that she is a technomancer, or a person who can access the internet without a device- because she is the device. Despite being a dryad, she uses this to her advantage, carefully attempting to hack the mega-ultra-corporations that have been rumoured to run a Dryad farm in the midst of the heavily forested areas of South America.

Unfortunately, because of her hacking partner, she is caught and- when we do start playing the campaign- she will start her new journey sat in a prison in yet another country, split from the child that she'd grown so attached to.
